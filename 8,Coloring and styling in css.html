Font Size

EXTRA SMALL-Used for footnotes, captions, or less important text. It saves space but should be readable.font-size: 10px;

SMALL-Used for body text on mobile or compact layouts; gives a neat and modern look.font-size: 14px;

MEDIUM-Default size for paragraph text on websites; easy to read and clear for most users.font-size: 16px;

LARGE-Used for subheadings, highlighting important content, or section titles.font-size: 20px;

EXTRA LARGE-Used for main headings or titles to grab attention and give structure to the page.font-size: 32px;



Font weight

LIGHT-For soft, modern designs or subtitles — looks elegant and clean.300

NORMAL(REGULAR)-Default text weight — perfect for body paragraphs and general reading.400

SEMI BOLD-Ideal for subheadings or highlighted text — gives balance between normal and bold.600

BOLD-Used for main headings or to emphasize important words or buttons.700.



2.TEXT STYLE SECTION

how your text looks or is presented — for example, whether it’s italic, bold, or uppercase.
In simple words: Text style changes the appearance or decoration of text.

Uppercase small label-Converts all letters to uppercase — used for labels or headings.text-transform: uppercase; font-size: small;

Muted descriptive text-Makes text look faded or less important — used for hints or descriptions.color: gray; opacity: 0.7;

Underlined link style -Adds an underline to text — often used for links.text-decoration: underline;

Struck-through text-Draws a line through text — used to show deleted or outdated content.text-decoration: line-through;

Highlighted text-Adds a background color to make the text stand out.background-color: yellow;

Text with subtle shadow-Adds a small shadow behind text for a soft 3D or glowing effect.text-shadow: 1px 1px 2px



3.BACKGROUND

In web design or styling, a background means the area or color behind your text, image, or content. It’s what fills the space behind an element (like a page, box, or button).

Solid Color-A single plain color — simple and clean.

Gradient-A smooth blend of two or more colors — gives a stylish look.

image-Uses a picture as the background.

Pattern / Texture-Repeats a small design to create a texture effect.

Transparent / NoneNo color — allows the layer below to show through.



4.Colours

Colouring means adding color to text, background, borders, or elements on a webpage to make it look attractive, clear, and readable. It helps users easily see what’s important and gives a beautiful design to your page.

box colouring

Box colouring means adding background color or border color to a section, div, button, or container (box). It helps separate areas and make a design look attractive.
